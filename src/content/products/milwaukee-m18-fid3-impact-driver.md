## In-Depth Look

The M18 FID3 represents Milwaukee's fourth-generation FUEL impact driver. At 226Nm of torque in a 121mm body, it delivers class-leading performance in a genuinely compact form factor — particularly useful for restricted-space work common in electrical, plumbing, and fit-out applications.

### 4-Mode DRIVE CONTROL

Mode 1 is a low-speed, reduced-torque setting for delicate fastening — small screws, soft materials, and precision work where overdriving is a risk. Mode 2 is calibrated for self-tapping screws into sheet metal, controlling the speed to prevent stripping as the screw seats. Mode 3 is the standard performance setting for most general fastening. Mode 4 delivers maximum torque for M8+ bolts, deck screws, structural fixings, and high-resistance applications. Selecting the correct mode prevents cam-out on small fasteners and extends the life of both bits and fixings.

### REDLINK PLUS Intelligence

REDLINK PLUS is Milwaukee's electronic management system that monitors motor temperature, current draw, and performance in real time. It protects the tool and battery from overloading and overheating under sustained high-torque applications — particularly relevant for electricians driving multiple M6 or M8 fixings into structural steelwork, where less sophisticated tools lose performance or shut down under thermal stress.

### Compact Length

At 121mm length, the FID3 is shorter than most impact drivers in its class. The difference over a standard 140–150mm body is significant in confined spaces — inside consumer unit enclosures, within stud bays, or driving fixings close to a wall or ceiling face. The compact design uses the same M18 FUEL motor and battery platform as full-size Milwaukee tools, with no performance compromise.

### Verdict

One of the best 18V impact drivers available. The 4-mode DRIVE CONTROL, REDLINK intelligence, and compact length make it equally capable for precision work and heavy structural fastening.
