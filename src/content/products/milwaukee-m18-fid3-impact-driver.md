The M18 FID3 represents Milwaukee's latest thinking in impact driver design. The combination of raw power, intelligent electronics, and compact form factor makes it one of the most capable impact drivers available.

Four selectable modes limit speed and torque output. The self-tapping screw mode is particularly useful for sheet metal work.
