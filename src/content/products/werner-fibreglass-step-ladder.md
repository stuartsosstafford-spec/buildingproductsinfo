## In-Depth Look

For electricians and anyone working near electrical installations, a fibreglass step ladder is an essential safety item rather than an optional upgrade. Aluminium is an excellent conductor of electricity; fibreglass is not. In situations where accidental contact with live conductors is possible — during first fix wiring, consumer unit work, or testing in occupied properties with live circuits — the non-conductive stiles provide a critical layer of protection that aluminium ladders cannot offer.

### EN 131 Professional Certification

The Werner 7160618 is certified to EN 131 Professional, the higher of the two EN 131 classifications. EN 131 Professional requires a 150kg load capacity (versus 100kg for the domestic classification) and is subject to more rigorous testing protocols for duty cycle and mechanical strength. For professional or commercial site use, EN 131 Professional is the appropriate specification. The domestic classification is adequate for infrequent homeowner use only.

### Fibreglass Stiles, Aluminium Treads

The stile material is the safety-critical element — fibreglass throughout, providing non-conductive performance even in damp or wet conditions. The treads are aluminium with slip-resistant surfaces, giving the durability and grip required for daily professional use. At 8.9kg, the ladder is heavier than an equivalent aluminium model (typically 5–6kg for a similar size), which is a practical trade-off for operatives carrying ladders regularly.

### Inspection and Maintenance

Fibreglass stiles must be kept clean and inspected regularly for surface damage, cracks, or contamination. Compromised fibreglass does not provide reliable insulation performance. Any ladder with damaged stiles should be taken out of service immediately. Werner provide replacement stile components for their professional ladder range.

### Verdict

Essential for any electrician working near live or potentially live conductors. The EN 131 Professional certification and 150kg load rating confirm it is built to genuine trade specification.
