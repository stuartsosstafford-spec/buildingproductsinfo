## In-Depth Look

The DHR242Z fills an important gap in the Makita 18V LXT range, providing genuine SDS+ capability without a mains cable. With 2.0 joules of impact energy, it handles everyday masonry drilling tasks comfortably.

### Anti-Vibration Technology

Makita's AVT system uses a counterbalance mechanism to reduce vibration transmitted to the operator, helping to stay within daily vibration exposure limits.

### Verdict

A practical cordless SDS+ that delivers genuine performance for everyday trade drilling tasks.
