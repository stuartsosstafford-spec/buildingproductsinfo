## In-Depth Look

The Bosch AHS 55-20 LI is a reliable cordless hedge trimmer from the Bosch garden division, designed for regular domestic hedge maintenance. It sits in the mid-range of the Bosch 18V cordless garden range, positioned for homeowners and gardeners rather than landscape contractors running multiple machines daily.

### 550mm Blade and 20mm Cutting Capacity

The 550mm blade suits medium-sized domestic hedges — longer than the 430mm blades found on entry-level trimmers, and adequate for most typical UK garden hedges. The 20mm maximum cutting diameter handles twiggy growth on established and regularly maintained hedges but will struggle on thick woody stems. Hedges that have been left uncut for several years may require initial tidying with a petrol trimmer or pruning saw before the AHS 55-20 can maintain them effectively.

### Anti-Blocking System

The anti-blocking system detects when the blade stalls on a thicker stem and temporarily reverses the blade direction to free the obstruction without manual intervention. This extends continuous cutting time on uneven hedges that include occasional thicker growth, reducing the stop-start interruptions that are common without this feature.

### Battery and Runtime

The 18V battery provides a single charge of typical runtime for an average domestic hedge trimming session. For larger gardens or multiple hedges in one session, a second battery is advisable. The battery is shared with other Bosch 18V garden tools including the Rotak cordless lawnmower range, making a second battery useful across the full range. Note that the AHS 55-20 LI takes the Bosch green (garden) range batteries, not the blue Bosch Professional 18V batteries — the two ranges are not cross-compatible.

### Verdict

A sensible, well-made cordless hedge trimmer for typical UK domestic gardens. The lightweight design, anti-blocking system, and adequate blade length cover most residential hedging requirements without the noise or hassle of a petrol machine.
