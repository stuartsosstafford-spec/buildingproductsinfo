## About Drayton

Drayton was founded in 1938 and has been a mainstay of UK heating controls for decades. Now part of Schneider Electric, the company manufactures thermostatic radiator valves, programmers, room thermostats, and smart heating controls from its UK base. Drayton products are found in millions of British homes and are widely specified by heating engineers for both new installations and system upgrades.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Thermostatic Radiator Valves**: The Wiser TRV range and traditional TRV4 for individual radiator temperature control
- **Programmers & Timers**: Single and multi-channel programmers for boiler and hot water scheduling
- **Room Thermostats**: Wired and wireless room stats for basic and advanced heating control
- **Smart Heating**: The Wiser smart heating system with app control, room-by-room scheduling, and energy insights

## Why Tradespeople Choose Drayton

Drayton is one of the most trusted names in UK heating controls, with products that heating engineers have been fitting for generations. The brand's TRVs and programmers are reliable, straightforward to install, and available at virtually every plumbing and heating merchant. The newer Wiser smart heating range gives installers a modern, easy-to-fit smart control option with strong manufacturer support.
