## About Hansgrohe

Hansgrohe was founded in 1901 in Schiltach, Germany, by Hans Grohe. The company has been at the forefront of bathroom technology for over a century, developing innovations including the first hand-held shower and the Ecostat thermostatic mixer range. Hansgrohe is the parent company of the Axor designer brand.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Ecostat**: Thermostatic shower mixers with SafetyStop technology
- **Croma**: Shower systems, heads, and rail kits
- **Focus & Metris**: Basin and kitchen mixers
- **Raindance**: Premium overhead shower systems

## Why Tradespeople Choose Hansgrohe

Hansgrohe is the brand that plumbers and bathroom installers recommend when clients want quality they can rely on. The thermostatic cartridges are precise and long-lasting, the chrome finish is consistently excellent, and the 5-year warranty reflects confidence in the product. Standard 150mm connections and straightforward installation also make them practical to fit.
