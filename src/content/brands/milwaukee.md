## About Milwaukee

Milwaukee Tool was founded in 1924 and has built a reputation for heavy-duty, professional-grade tools. The brand is part of Techtronic Industries (TTI) and has seen significant growth in the UK market, particularly among M&E contractors and specialist trades.

## Key Product Ranges

- **M18 FUEL Platform**: 18V brushless cordless tools with REDLINK intelligence
- **M12 FUEL Platform**: Compact 12V cordless tools for tight spaces
- **Hand Tools**: Pliers, cutters, measuring tools, and storage
- **ONE-KEY**: Connected tool technology for tracking and customisation

## Why Tradespeople Choose Milwaukee

Milwaukee has built a strong following among UK tradespeople who need heavy-duty performance. The M18 FUEL range is particularly popular in mechanical and electrical trades, where the combination of power, compact size, and REDLINK protection provides genuine productivity advantages.
