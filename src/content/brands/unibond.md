## About UniBond

UniBond is one of the most recognised adhesive and sealant brands in the UK, owned by the German multinational Henkel. The brand offers a wide range of products for bonding, sealing, filling, and tiling, serving both the trade and DIY markets. UniBond products are widely available across builders' merchants, DIY sheds, and trade counters throughout the country.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Adhesives**: General-purpose, grab, and specialist adhesives for wood, metal, plastic, and construction materials
- **Sealants**: Silicone, acrylic, and hybrid sealants for bathrooms, kitchens, and general construction joints
- **Tiling Products**: Tile adhesives, grouts, and preparation products for wall and floor tiling
- **Fillers & Repair**: Wall fillers, repair compounds, and surface preparation products

## Why Tradespeople Choose UniBond

UniBond is a safe choice for UK tradespeople because of its wide availability, consistent quality, and broad product range. The brand's sealants and adhesives are well-known for reliable performance, and their presence in virtually every builders' merchant means tradespeople can pick up what they need without having to seek out specialist suppliers.
