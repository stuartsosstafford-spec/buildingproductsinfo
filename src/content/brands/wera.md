## About Wera

Wera was founded in 1936 in Wuppertal, Germany, and has built a strong reputation for producing innovative, high-performance hand tools. The brand is best known for its screwdrivers and hex keys, which feature distinctive ergonomic designs and patented technologies aimed at reducing cam-out, improving grip, and making repetitive fastening tasks faster and more comfortable.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Screwdrivers**: Kraftform, Kraftform Kompakt, and VDE-rated screwdrivers with ergonomic handles and anti-roll features
- **Hex Keys**: Colour-coded hex key sets with ball-end and holding function options
- **Bit Sets & Ratchets**: Zyklop ratchets, Bit-Check sets, and Impaktor impact-rated bits
- **Tool Sets**: Organised multi-tool sets and pouches for electricians, plumbers, and general trades

## Why Tradespeople Choose Wera

Wera has developed a loyal following among UK tradespeople, particularly electricians and mechanical trades, thanks to the comfort, durability, and clever engineering of its tools. Features such as the Lasertip screwdriver point, which grips the screw head to reduce slippage, and the Rapidaptor quick-release bit holder demonstrate the brand's focus on solving everyday frustrations on site.
