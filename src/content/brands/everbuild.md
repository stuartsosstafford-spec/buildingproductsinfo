## About Everbuild

Everbuild was founded in 1982 in Leeds and has grown into one of the UK's leading manufacturers of sealants, adhesives, and building chemicals. Acquired by the Sika group, Everbuild continues to operate from its Yorkshire base and supplies an extensive catalogue of products to builders' merchants and trade outlets across the country, with its distinctive red branding well known on UK job sites.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Sealants**: Silicone, acrylic, and hybrid sealants for construction, glazing, and sanitary applications
- **Adhesives**: Grab adhesives, PVA, contact adhesives, and specialist bonding products
- **Building Chemicals**: DPCs, waterproofers, brick and patio cleaners, and surface treatments
- **Fillers & Repair**: Interior and exterior fillers, caulks, and repair compounds

## Why Tradespeople Choose Everbuild

Everbuild is a firm favourite with UK builders and general tradespeople thanks to its wide range of competitively priced construction consumables. Products like the Forever White silicone sealant and the 502 All Purpose Weatherproof Wood Adhesive have become site staples, and the brand's consistent availability through builders' merchants makes it a convenient and reliable choice for everyday construction needs.
