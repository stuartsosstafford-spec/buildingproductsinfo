## About Bosch Professional

Bosch Professional (distinguished by its blue livery) is the trade and industrial tool division of the Bosch Group, one of the world's largest engineering companies. Founded in 1886, Bosch has been manufacturing power tools since 1932.

## Key Product Ranges

- **18V Professional Platform**: Cordless tools including drills, grinders, saws, and more
- **BITURBO Platform**: High-performance brushless tools
- **Measuring Technology**: Laser measures, levels, and detection tools
- **Accessories**: Drill bits, saw blades, and abrasives

## Why Tradespeople Choose Bosch Professional

Bosch Professional tools are widely respected for their build quality and engineering. The brand has a strong presence in the UK trade market, particularly in measuring and detection technology where Bosch laser measures and stud detectors are market leaders.
