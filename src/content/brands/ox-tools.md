## About OX Tools

OX Tools was founded in Australia and has grown rapidly in the UK market, becoming a well-known brand among tradespeople in the building and finishing trades. The company offers a tiered product range — Trade, Pro, and Speedskim — designed to meet different budget and performance requirements, with a strong focus on tools for plastering, bricklaying, tiling, and general site work.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Plastering Tools**: Finishing trowels, floats, hawks, and the Speedskim range for fast, flat plaster finishes
- **Bricklaying Tools**: Brick trowels, jointers, lines, and levels for masonry work
- **Cutting & Grinding**: Diamond blades, tile cutters, and cutting discs
- **PPE & Site Accessories**: Safety boots, gloves, knee pads, and general site equipment

## Why Tradespeople Choose OX Tools

OX Tools has built a strong following among UK bricklayers and plasterers in particular, thanks to well-designed tools at competitive prices. The Speedskim range has been especially popular, offering a modern approach to plaster finishing that saves time on site. The brand's tiered product levels allow tradespeople to choose between value and premium options depending on their needs.
