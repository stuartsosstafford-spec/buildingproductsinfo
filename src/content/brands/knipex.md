## About Knipex

Knipex was founded in 1882 in Wuppertal, Germany, and has remained focused on a single product category — pliers and cutting tools — for over 140 years. All Knipex products are manufactured in Germany, and the brand is widely considered the industry standard for plier quality among professional tradespeople across Europe and the UK.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Combination & Cutting Pliers**: High-leverage combination pliers, diagonal cutters, and side cutters
- **Water Pump Pliers**: The Cobra range of adjustable water pump pliers, a bestseller among plumbers and electricians
- **VDE Insulated Tools**: Fully insulated pliers and cutters rated to 1000V for safe electrical work
- **Specialist Pliers**: Circlip pliers, electronics pliers, and pipe grips for specific trade applications

## Why Tradespeople Choose Knipex

Knipex pliers are a premium choice that UK electricians, plumbers, and mechanical tradespeople invest in for their precision, durability, and long-term performance. The Cobra water pump pliers in particular have become an essential tool on many UK job sites, and the brand's commitment to manufacturing everything in Germany gives professionals confidence in consistent quality across the range.
