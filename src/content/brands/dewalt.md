## About DeWalt

DeWalt was founded in 1923 by Raymond E. DeWalt, who invented the radial arm saw. Today, it is one of the most recognised tool brands on construction sites worldwide and is part of the Stanley Black & Decker corporation.

## Key Product Ranges

- **18V XR Platform**: Cordless power tools including drills, impact drivers, saws, and grinders
- **54V FlexVolt**: Higher-powered cordless tools for demanding applications
- **Hand Tools**: Measuring tapes, levels, knives, and site accessories
- **Storage**: TSTAK and ToughSystem tool storage solutions

## Why Tradespeople Choose DeWalt

DeWalt has built a strong reputation in the UK trade market through consistent product quality, wide availability at trade counters, and an extensive cordless platform that allows users to share batteries across a large range of tools.
