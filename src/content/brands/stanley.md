## About Stanley

Stanley was founded in 1843 in New Britain, Connecticut, and is one of the oldest and most recognised hand tool brands in the world. Now part of the Stanley Black & Decker corporation alongside brands such as DeWalt and Black & Decker, Stanley continues to supply a comprehensive range of hand tools, measuring instruments, and storage solutions used by tradespeople and DIY enthusiasts across the UK.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Measuring & Layout**: Tape measures including the iconic FatMax range, spirit levels, and laser measurers
- **Cutting Tools**: Retractable knives, fixed-blade knives, and replacement blade systems
- **Hand Tools**: Hammers, chisels, screwdrivers, and pliers for general construction and joinery
- **Tool Storage**: Tool bags, boxes, and the FatMax mobile work stations

## Why Tradespeople Choose Stanley

Stanley has been a fixture on UK construction sites for generations, with products like the FatMax tape measure becoming industry standards. The brand's hand tools offer dependable performance at accessible price points, and the sheer breadth of the range means tradespeople can equip themselves with trusted essentials from a single, widely available brand.
