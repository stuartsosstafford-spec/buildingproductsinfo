## About Rothenberger

Rothenberger was founded in 1949 in Germany and has established itself as a leading manufacturer of specialist plumbing and pipework tools. The company supplies professional plumbers, heating engineers, and mechanical contractors with a comprehensive range of tools for pipe cutting, bending, threading, pressing, and jointing, and is widely recognised across the UK plumbing trade.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Pipe Cutting & Preparation**: Pipe cutters, reamers, and deburring tools for copper, plastic, and steel
- **Pipe Bending**: Manual and hydraulic pipe benders for various pipe materials and diameters
- **Press Fitting Tools**: Battery and mains-powered pressing tools for modern push-fit and press-fit systems
- **Soldering & Brazing**: Torches, gas cylinders, and soldering accessories for traditional pipework jointing

## Why Tradespeople Choose Rothenberger

Rothenberger is a go-to brand for UK plumbers and heating engineers who need dependable, specialist pipework tools. The brand's focus on the plumbing and mechanical trades means its products are purpose-built for the specific demands of pipe installation, offering precision and durability that general tool brands often cannot match.
