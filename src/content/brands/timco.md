## About TIMCO

TIMCO is a leading UK distributor of fixings, fasteners, and building products, offering an extensive catalogue of construction consumables. The company supplies trade merchants throughout the United Kingdom with a comprehensive range of screws, nails, anchors, and associated building products, making it a familiar name at trade counters nationwide.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Screws & Fixings**: Wood screws, self-drilling screws, coach bolts, and multi-purpose fixings
- **Nails & Pins**: Collated nails, lost head nails, and brads for a range of applications
- **Anchors & Plugs**: Wall plugs, chemical anchors, and heavy-duty fixings for masonry and plasterboard
- **Building Products**: Ventilation, fire protection, and general building accessories

## Why Tradespeople Choose TIMCO

TIMCO has become a trusted name in the UK trade market by providing a reliable, well-packaged range of fixings and fasteners that are readily available through builders' merchants. Their clear product labelling and consistent quality make it easy for tradespeople to find the right fixing for the job without the guesswork.
