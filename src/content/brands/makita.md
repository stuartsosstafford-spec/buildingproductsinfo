## About Makita

Founded in 1915 in Nagoya, Japan, Makita began as an electric motor sales and repair company before moving into power tool manufacturing. Today, Makita is one of the world's largest power tool manufacturers, with a product range spanning cordless tools, outdoor power equipment, and pneumatic tools.

## Key Product Ranges

- **18V LXT Platform**: The core of Makita's cordless range with over 300 products
- **40V Max XGT**: Higher-powered cordless tools for demanding applications
- **Outdoor Power Equipment**: Lawn mowers, chainsaws, blowers, and hedge trimmers
- **Accessories**: Drill bits, saw blades, sanding discs, and consumables

## Why Tradespeople Choose Makita

Makita's 18V LXT platform is one of the most extensive in the industry, allowing tradespeople to run a complete cordless toolkit on a single battery system. The brand is particularly popular among electricians and plumbers for its compact tool designs and reliability.
