## About Grohe

Grohe was founded in 1936 in Hemer, Germany, and has grown into one of the world's leading manufacturers of sanitary fittings and bathroom products. Now part of the Japanese LIXIL group, Grohe is known for combining German engineering with thoughtful design, supplying taps, showers, flushing systems, and kitchen fittings that are widely specified by UK plumbers, bathroom installers, and architects.

## Key Product Ranges

- **Bathroom Taps & Mixers**: Basin, bath, and bidet mixers in a wide range of styles and finishes
- **Showers**: Shower systems, thermostatic valves, heads, and rails for domestic and commercial installations
- **Kitchen Fittings**: Kitchen mixer taps including filtered and boiling water systems
- **Flushing Systems**: Concealed cisterns, flush plates, and WC frames for modern bathroom installations

## Why Tradespeople Choose Grohe

Grohe is widely trusted by UK plumbers and bathroom installers for the reliability of its products and the consistency of its quality across the range. The brand's strong support network, wide availability through plumbing merchants, and comprehensive technical documentation make it a straightforward choice for professionals who need fittings that install cleanly and perform reliably over the long term.
